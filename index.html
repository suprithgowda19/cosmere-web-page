Digitisation Solutions for AEC & Infrastructure Industries

We at Cosmere are firm believers that technology can be used to increase the productivity of AEC and Survey industry and improve project outcomes both in terms of cost and time. Infrastructure digitization is the way forward with digital construction, mapping, and measurement. Cosmere Technologies provides digital engineering and specialist modelling services to the global AECO industry. We provide BIM, Geospatial, Digitization and CGI services. Our team leverages software tools and domain knowledge to provide services that are of true value.

Reliable provider to global AEC companies

We bring together the right people & right technology at the right cost. We at Cosmere are a reliable partner for Architects, Engineers & Contractors in 25+ countries helping over 100+ clients. Over the past 5 years, we have delivered a wide range of projects across utilities, infrastructure, airports, hospital, hospitality, religious, industrial, commercial and residential sectors.
